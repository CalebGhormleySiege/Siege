# Game Summary
Siege is a Survival, Tower Defense, City Builder game that focuses on surviving a siege until a relief army arrives, by defending your Star Fortress through successful utilization of your people and resources. The style of gameplay is similar to Frostpunk, in that assigning jobs and building are the primary player actions, and in the increasing desperation as conditions deteriorate while you try to hold the fortress.

# Game Structure
The game is broken up into sieges called Campaigns, in which the player is either defending or besieging a renaissance-era star fortress. Campaigns are broken up into 4 act story structures in which new elements are introduced (weather, assault or sortie types, plague, etc.) over the course of the act and then come together during a major assault at the end of the act to create a difficult climax at the end of each act.
